about

Why I build it this way.

I started out fixing other people's rushed MVPs — most of the problems traced back to unclear scope, not bad code. So I work backward from your business outcome first, and let that decide the technical approach, not the other way around.

how I work

Small, shippable milestones with a staging link you can check at any time — you'll never be waiting on a surprise reveal.

communication

One weekly update, async by default, with a same-day reply window on anything blocking. No status-meeting theater.

development philosophy

Ship the smallest version that proves the idea, then add complexity only when real usage asks for it.

what to expect

A written scope before any code, honest timelines, and a heads-up the moment something looks like it'll slip — not after.
